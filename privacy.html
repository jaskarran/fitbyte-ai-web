Privacy Policy for FitByte AI

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and contact details
  • Age, gender, height, weight, and other health metrics
  • Dietary preferences, restrictions, and fitness goals
  • Meal plans, nutrition data, and fitness activity information

1.2 Usage Data

  • App usage statistics and interaction data
  • Device information (model, OS version, unique device identifiers)
  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)

1.3 Health Data

  • Workout routines and exercise data
  • Nutrition and meal tracking information
  • Sleep patterns and recovery metrics (if applicable)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To personalize your experience and meal plans
  • To improve our app and develop new features
  • To communicate with you about updates and offers
  • To analyze app usage and user behavior
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our services

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our app
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • In connection with business transfers or acquisitions

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication measures

5. Your Data Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and for legitimate business purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

For any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@fitbyte.ai
